My Approach

01

Research First

Before writing, I study the prospect — their content, language, offer, and pain points. Every message feels like it was written for that one person.

02

Open the Door

The first message never pitches. It creates curiosity — a specific observation, a relevant question, an insight they haven't heard before.

03

Add Value

Every follow-up gives something new — an insight, a case study, a question. Never just a "bump." Conversations build trust, not pressure.

04

Convert & Report

Weekly reporting on open rates, replies, and conversions. Full transparency. Campaigns improve with every send based on real data.

Auto loan approval service targeting people with bad credit. Full 7-email nurture sequence from cold awareness to conversion.

Email 1 — Subject Line
"Dream car and credit score…"
Opening
Ever wonder how closely knit the process of buying your dream car and improving your credit score really is? With bad credit, you are unable to get approved for auto loans from banks or car dealerships… Maybe you already know that, but the real challenge is improving your credit score… After all, it can take months (sometimes years) to improve your credit score. There are dozens of different steps involved and the entire process is daunting, to say the least…
↳ Sequence runs across 7 emails: Awareness → Education → Social Proof → Objection Handling → Urgency → CTA
